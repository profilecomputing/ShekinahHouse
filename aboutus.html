About Us

Offering hope and providing services and support for the homeless

  • Incorporated in Dec 2014. Registered as a charity with the ACNC
  • A local, grass-roots organisation, providing a range of services and support for those who are homeless, particularly those who are sleeping rough.
  • Also offering a smaller suite of services and support for those who are financially and /or socially disadvantaged.
  • Fully staffed by a dedicated team of volunteers
  • Financed by in-house fundraising and community support through donations and fundraising without recurring Government funding.
